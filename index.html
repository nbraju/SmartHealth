Daily Voice Check-in

Every morning AI asks “How are you feeling?” — just say “Good” or “Not well”. If negative, a nurse or family gets automatic alert.

Medication Reminders

Smart voice reminders + optional Bluetooth pill box. “Take your BP tablet?” – simple yes/no reply via WhatsApp or app.

Vital Signs Tracking

Sync BP monitor, glucose meter via voice upload or Bluetooth. Family sees trends & early warnings 5–7 days before emergency.

Emergency SOS + Fall Detection

Press big red SOS button or automatic fall detection (wearable). Instantly alerts family & nearest hospital.

WhatsApp First Integration

Seniors already use WhatsApp! Receive reminders, reply “Yes” or voice note. No learning curve.

Biometric Login

Fingerprint / face login – no passwords to forget. One touch access to health dashboard.

Family Caregiver Dashboard

Adult children get daily summaries via WhatsApp: “Mom’s BP normal, medicines taken ✅”. Peace of mind.

AI Companion for Loneliness

Emotional support chat (voice-based) that checks mood, shares jokes, and reduces isolation — never paywalled.
